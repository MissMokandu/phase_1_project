Tips

  • Know your needs: City driving? Family travel? Off-road? Start there.
  • Set a budget: Include fuel, insurance, repairs — not just the price tag.
  • Test drive: How it *feels* matters as much as features.
  • Research reviews: Real experiences help you avoid common regrets.
  • Check history (for used cars): Service records, mileage, and accident reports.
